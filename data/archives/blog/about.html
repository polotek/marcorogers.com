About Marco Rogers

I work as web developer for Atlantic Media Company in Washington D.C. I like to think I'm pretty proficient with html, css and javascript. I've worked in several languages on the back-end including php, python, java, C# and even tcl.

I've been building dynamic, content-driven web sites for over 4 years. I'm always on the lookout for ways to make it easier. I've worked with several frameworks and content management systems. They have all been found lacking to one degree or another. But I keep soldiering forward. My fondest dream is to one day design and optimize myself out of a job.

I also love to watch, talk about and generally criticize movies. See below.
